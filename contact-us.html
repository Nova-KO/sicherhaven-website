01

When will Eventify be available?

Eventify is currently in active development and we expect to launch the beta version in early 2025. We are focusing on creating a seamless user experience for both event organizers and attendees, with features like real-time event discovery, easy registration, and community engagement tools.

02

What features will WealthWise include?

WealthWise will be an AI-powered financial wellness application that helps users understand their financial health through credit monitoring, spending analysis, fee detection, and personalized financial insights. The app will provide actionable recommendations to improve financial literacy and reduce unnecessary expenses.

03

How can I stay updated on your product releases?

You can stay updated on our product development progress by subscribing to our newsletter, following us on social media, or contacting us directly. We regularly share updates about Eventify's development milestones and WealthWise's feature planning on our website and through email communications.

04

Do you offer early access or beta testing opportunities?

Yes! We are actively seeking beta testers for Eventify and welcome early feedback from potential users. If you're interested in participating in our beta program or providing input on WealthWise's development, please reach out to us through our contact page. Your feedback is invaluable in shaping our products.
