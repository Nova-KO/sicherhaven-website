About EventifyPlus

EventifyPlus (also known as Eventify by SicherHaven) is the flagship community product of SicherHaven Technologies Private Limited. Launched in 2025, the platform was developed with a vision to "bridge the gap between local communities and meaningful experiences." It operates primarily through social media channels like Instagram (@eventify.plus) and a dedicated application, positioning itself as the "ultimate guide" to Kerala's diverse events landscape.

Celebrating Kerala's Culture

The platform is distinctively curated to showcase the vibrancy of "God's Own Country." It features extensive coverage of traditional and hyperlocal events, ranging from major spectacles like the Thrissur Pooram and Onam celebrations to niche cultural performances like Kathakali and Theyyam. By documenting these events with high-quality visuals and updates, EventifyPlus aims to preserve and promote the state's cultural identity for both locals and tourists.

Community and Utility

Beyond just listing events, EventifyPlus serves as a dual-sided marketplace. For attendees, it offers a streamlined way to discover "what's happening near me," from boat races in the backwaters to Christmas carnivals in Kochi. For event hosts and organizers, it provides a digital avenue to reach a broader audience, helping them publicize local gatherings that might otherwise rely solely on word-of-mouth.

Key Features

  • Hyperlocal Event Discovery: Curates a wide range of local events specific to Kerala, from major cultural festivals like Thrissur Pooram and Onam to niche community gatherings and temple festivities.
  • Visual-First Interface: Leverages high-quality visuals, reels, and stories to showcase the vibrancy of events, effectively serving as a digital "lens" on Kerala's cultural landscape.
  • Dual-Sided Community Platform: Functions as a two-way marketplace where attendees find events and organizers gain a dedicated channel to publicize their events.
  • Comprehensive Digital Guide: Beyond dates and times, acts as a cultural guide offering context and details that help preserve and promote Kerala's destination identity.
